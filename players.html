His humor may be as dry and sarcastic as any Brits', but that doesnt mean he isn't a well-meaning one. In game, Cloudy is a medic main as you want him - focusing on self-preservation above all else, calling when to go in or back off his always aware of both the game AND the metagame (as in being aware of UBER advantage and taking the updated number from the spy(metagame) while trying to survive against the enemy combo (game)). His calls are rather decent and the one weakness he has are days of bad luck when he gets getting caught off-guard.
